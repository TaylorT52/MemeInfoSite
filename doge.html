Doge

The word "doge" was originally a misspelling of the word "dog." As a meme, it is often used to refer to a particular photo of a species of dog Shiba Inus (nicknamed "Shibe"). The meme is often accompanied by satirical captions of an internal monologue, in comic sans font.

The meme was prominent around 2010-2014, and many variations in different situations were created (see slideshow at right). The meme became so popular that a meme cryptocurrency called Dogecoin was created in 2013.

The meme was even incorporated into a joke game called Doge 2048.
