Dabbing

Dabbing is a move in which one points one arm to the sky while burying their head in their other arm as if they are sneezing. Dabbing originated in 2015 when it was first created by the Atlanta based rapping group Migos. It was popularized by famous football players such as Cam Newton and took the world by storm. People around the world began dabbing as a celebration for various feats, including landing “bottle flips,” another trend of the time. Dabbing eventually devolved into the universal stereotype of a cringey move that little kids did, forever enshrining it as one of 2016’s funniest internet memes.
